\# 🎬 Smart Slideshow Maker



A simple desktop app for creating \*\*automatic image slideshows with motion, transitions, and audio\*\*.



Turn a folder of images into a polished video — no editing experience required.



---



\## ✨ Features



\* 📸 Add images or entire folders

\* 🎞️ Automatic slideshow creation based on target duration

\* 🎥 Smooth transitions:



&nbsp; \* Crossfade

&nbsp; \* Slide Left / Right

&nbsp; \* Dip to Black

\* 🌀 Motion effects (zoom + pan / Ken Burns style)

\* 🎵 Add background music:



&nbsp; \* Single track loop

&nbsp; \* Playlist (once or looped)

\* 🎯 Smart image selection (clarity + uniqueness)

\* ⚙️ Custom export settings (resolution, FPS, duration)

\* 🖥️ Built-in preview



---

```bash

```

Navigate to the project folder and run:




---



\## 🖱️ How to Use



\### 1. Add images



\* Click \*\*"Add Images"\*\* or \*\*"Add Folder"\*\*



\### 2. (Optional) Add audio



\* Click \*\*"Add Track(s)"\*\* or \*\*"Add Audio Folder"\*\*



\### 3. Adjust settings



\* Resolution (e.g. Full HD)

\* Target duration (e.g. 90 seconds)

\* Transition style

\* Motion intensity



\### 4. Choose output file



\* Click \*\*"Browse"\*\* and select where to save the video



\### 5. Export



\* Click \*\*"Export Slideshow"\*\*



---



\## 📁 Supported Formats



\### Images



\* `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.bmp`, `.webp`, `.tif`, `.tiff`



\### Audio



\* `.mp3`, `.wav`, `.m4a`, `.aac`, `.flac`, `.ogg`, `.wma`



---



\## ⚙️ How It Works (Overview)



The app automatically:



\* Selects the best images based on clarity and uniqueness

\* Applies motion effects (zoom and pan)

\* Adds transitions between images

\* Matches everything to your chosen video length

\* Exports a finished slideshow video



---



\## 💡 Tips



\* Use high-quality images for best results

\* Longer target durations allow more images to be included

\* Adding audio improves the final video experience
